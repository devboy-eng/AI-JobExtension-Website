Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2024 | Last Updated: January 1, 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

KUPOSU AI is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. We only collect data necessary to provide our AI-powered resume customization services and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how KUPOSU AI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our website, Chrome extension, and related services. Please read this policy carefully.

By using KUPOSU AI services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when:

  • Creating an account
  • Setting up your profile
  • Using our resume customization services
  • Contacting customer support
  • Making payments
Information Type Examples Purpose
Account Information Name, email address, password Account creation and management
Profile Data Work experience, education, skills, contact details Resume customization and optimization
Payment Information Billing address, transaction details (via secure payment providers) Payment processing and fraud prevention
Communication Data Support requests, feedback, inquiries Customer support and service improvement

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: How you interact with our extension and website
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Log Information: IP address, access times, pages viewed
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences, analytics

2.3 Job Posting Data

When you use our extension on job platforms, we may collect:

  • Job descriptions and requirements
  • Company names and job titles
  • Job posting URLs (for matching purposes only)

Note: We do not store complete job postings permanently and only use this data for resume optimization.

  • Service Provision: Customize resumes based on job requirements
  • AI Processing: Analyze job descriptions and optimize resume content
  • Account Management: Maintain your account and preferences
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and provide assistance
  • Payment Processing: Handle transactions and billing

3.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI algorithms
  • Enhance user experience and interface design
  • Develop new features and capabilities
  • Monitor and improve system performance

3.3 Communication

  • Send important service updates and announcements
  • Provide customer support and technical assistance
  • Share product updates and new features (optional)
  • Send security alerts and account notifications

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Never Sell Your Data

KUPOSU AI does not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4.1 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

4.2 Service Providers

  • Analytics: Usage analytics for service improvement

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in good faith belief that such disclosure is necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations or court orders

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the business transaction, subject to the same privacy protections.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in encrypted databases with access controls
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations
  • Regular Audits: Ongoing security assessments and improvements
  • Employee Training: Staff trained on data protection best practices

5.2 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data deleted after 2 years of inactivity
  • Job Processing Data: Temporary data deleted within 30 days
  • Payment Records: Retained for 7 years for legal compliance

5.3 Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required
  • Provide detailed information about the incident
  • Take immediate steps to secure the system

6.1 Account Controls

  • Access: View and download your personal data
  • Update: Modify your profile information anytime
  • Delete: Request account deletion and data removal
  • Export: Download your resume data in standard formats

6.2 Privacy Controls

  • Marketing Emails: Opt out of promotional communications
  • Data Processing: Request limitation of data processing
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Corrections: Request correction of inaccurate data

6.3 Cookie Management

You can control cookie settings through your browser:

  • Accept or reject cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

7. Third-Party Services

7.1 Job Platforms

Our extension operates on third-party job platforms (LinkedIn, Naukri). We:

  • Only access publicly available job posting information
  • Do not store your login credentials for these platforms
  • Comply with each platform's terms of service
  • Do not modify or interfere with platform functionality

7.2 External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7.3 AI Services

We use external AI services for resume optimization:

  • Data sent to AI services is anonymized when possible
  • We have data processing agreements with AI providers
  • No personal identifiers are shared unnecessarily
  • AI-processed data is not used to train external models

KUPOSU AI is based in India. If you access our services from other countries, please note that:

  • Your information may be transferred to and processed in India
  • We ensure adequate protection for international transfers
  • Data protection standards equivalent to your country are maintained
  • You consent to such transfers by using our services

KUPOSU AI services are not designed for or directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete it immediately.

If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately at hello@kuposu.co.

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

10.1 Your Rights

  • Right to Know: Request information about data collection and use
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices

10.2 Exercising Rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@kuposu.co with your request. We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

11. European Union Privacy Rights

If you are in the EU, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Lawful Basis: We process data based on consent and legitimate interests
  • Data Protection Officer: Available for privacy-related inquiries
  • Supervisory Authority: Right to lodge complaints with data protection authorities
  • Automated Decision Making: Limited automated processing with human review options

12. Cancellations and Refunds

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team:

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:
